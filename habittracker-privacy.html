Habit Tracker: Pro | Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Privacy First Approach

Habit Tracker: Pro is designed with privacy as a core principle. All your habit data stays on your device - we never collect, access, or transmit any personal information.

Data We Collect

Habit Tracker: Pro stores all your habit data locally on your device. We do not collect, access, or transmit any personal information to our servers or third parties.

Local Data Storage

  • All habit names, completion dates, and settings are stored securely on your device
  • No data leaves your device or gets sent to external servers
  • Your information is private and only accessible to you
  • Data is stored using iOS secure storage mechanisms

In-App Purchases

We use Apple's In-App Purchase system for premium features. Apple handles all payment processing according to their privacy policy. We do not store or access your payment information.

Third-Party Services

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your usage of the app is completely private.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data by:

  • Using the "Clear All Data" option in the app settings
  • Deleting the app from your device

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for children's data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
